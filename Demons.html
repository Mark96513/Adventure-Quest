Demons

Description

Demons are the destroyers of civilisations. They are evil spirits, if they are able to possess a human being, they will turn into nightmarish creatures.

Living burning hell, from the ashes rises a powerful demon. They are huge and powerful. They can cause earthquakes if so willing.

They have high armor which makes them durable in combat.
