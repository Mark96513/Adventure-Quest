The beautiful angel princess

Story

Long time ago there was a beautiful princess called Aurora. She lived happily and freely. She was the most beautiful angel princess to have ever lived and was adored by everyone she meets.

One night, when she was fast asleep in the Castle, a witch appeared before her. The witch saw how beautiful she was, he wanted to keep her forever.

The witch spinned his cane 3 times and whispered some dark magic spells. A vortex then appeared, where it captured the angel princess, the witch besides her.

The angel princess let out a loud scream "Helpppppp Meeeeeeeee!" to which woke everyone in the city! Her cry for help was heard before she disappeared with the witch. Can you help save and find the beautiful angel princess?

Choose your Faith: Angel, Demon, Vampire, Werewolf

Choose Wisely

Upon the relevelation of the princess missing, the officials made a cry of plea for someone to help. And then..

4 supernatural beings set foot in the city. They offered to convert anyone who wishes to become them.

They were:

  • Mark the Angel,
  • Allen the Demon,
  • Josh the Werewolf
  • and Michael the Vampire.

To become one of them was the sacrifice you have to make, to be powerful enough to defeat the Witch and its dominions, and ultimately save the wondrous angel princess.
