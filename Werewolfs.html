Werewolfs

Description

Shapeshifters. As a man or as a wolf, werewolfs devastates enemies with it sharp claws and deadly jaws.

Brave and loyal, they will fight to the death if needed.

They have abilities to call on mother nature when their full potential is unlock! Can make armies!

From the depths of their human bodies lies an animal waiting to be unleashed! "SNAP! CRACKLE! POP!" Emerges the beast!

Statistics

Attributes:

  • Heath 720, Mana 351
  • Strength scale 9/10
  • Agility speed 9/10 as Humans 1/10
  • Intelligence 9/10

Weakness

Can only turn once every three days as it takes them time to heal.
In this time they are vulnerable. Also they are weakened by any Iron material. Natural enemies include:

  • Other clans of Werewolfs
  • Vampires

To become one of them is the sacrifice you have to make, to be powerful enough to defeat the Witch and its dominions, and ultimately save the wondrous angel princess!
